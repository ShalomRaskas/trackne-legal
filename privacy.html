PRIVACY POLICY

The policy, without the fog.

LAST UPDATED · APR 24, 2026

THE SHORT VERSION

We keep your data private. Never sold, never shared with advertisers. Delete everything with one tap. Photos are encrypted and tied only to your account.

01 Information we collect
  • Account info: email and name (if provided)
  • Onboarding responses: age range, skin type, concerns, goals
  • Progress photos taken within the app
  • Routine logs and trigger journal entries
  • Anonymous crash reports and performance data
02 How we use your data
  • Run your personal routine tracker and progress timeline
  • Generate AI routine suggestions and monthly reviews via Anthropic's Claude API (your data is not used to train models)
  • Send the reminders you configure
  • Fix bugs and improve the app
03 What we never do
  • Sell your data to advertisers, brokers, or anyone
  • Share photos with anyone outside your account
  • Use photos or journal entries to train AI models
  • Show third-party ads based on your data
04 Where your data lives
  • Photos encrypted in cloud storage (Supabase, SOC 2 certified)
  • Account data in a private Postgres database, encrypted at rest
  • Photos accessible only to you via your authenticated account
05 Your rights
  • Export: download all data as a JSON + photo bundle (Settings → Privacy)
  • Delete: permanently erase all data, including photos, with one tap
  • Access: see exactly what we have about you, anytime
  • Correction: update anything that's wrong
06 Age requirement

Trackne is intended for users 13 and older. We don't knowingly collect from children under 13. If you believe we have, email shalomrask@gmail.com and we'll delete it.

07 Questions?

Email shalomrask@gmail.com. We respond within 48 hours.
